Memory Card Game with Russian Dolls!

How to play the game

To begin with, press the Start Game button. Next, flip over two cards on the board. If the cards match, keep flipping until you uncover all the cards. To restart the game, press Reset Game button. You need to restart the game after you have finished it. Whoever makes the fewer points and/or completes in the least time to uncover all the cards, wins.
